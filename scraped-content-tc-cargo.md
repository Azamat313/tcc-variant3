**LOGISTICS**

TransCaspian Cargo provides comprehensive management of international transportation along key transport corridors in Central Asia, the Caspian region, China, Europe and the Middle East. We specialize in multimodal and transit schemes, as well as in the maintenance of complex cargo routes, ensuring reliability, predictability and transparency at every stage of cargo movement.

**OUR COMPETENCIES:**
- Development of the optimal route and selection of the type of transport, taking into account the specifics of the cargo, timing and infrastructure constraints.
- Calculation of the cost and time of transportation, preparation of several alternative logistics solutions.
- Preparation and processing of transport and customs documentation, including international accompanying documents.
- Organization of rail, road, sea and multimodal transportation, including connections and terminal services.
- Control of loading and unloading operations, ensuring technological compliance.

## Course Title
BRI LOGISTICS: ИНФРАСТРУКТУРА, РИСКИ И СЦЕНАРИИ РАЗВИТИЯ ЦЕНТРАЛЬНОЙ АЗИИ

## Description
A strategic course designed for specialists, managers, and analysts working in international logistics across Central Asia.

The course aims to help participants form an international competence system in Central Asia in the field of analysis, design and management of logistics corridors and export routes, based on the standards of ADB, AIIB, EBRD, OECD, UNESCAP and the principles of the OECD Learning Compass 2030.

## Course Format
- Duration: 24 academic hours
- Format: Hybrid (lectures, case studies, simulations, project work)
- Level: Professional — for directors, analysts, experts, and educators
- Certificate: Confirms professional development and readiness for international-level work

## ЧЕМУ ВЫ НАУЧИТЕСЬ (What You Will Learn)
- Master route efficiency assessment tools (ROI, KPI, ESG Dashboard)
- Design logistics routes accounting for risks, infrastructure, and tariffs
- Analyze route effectiveness and development scenarios through 2035
- Apply ESG approaches and blended finance instruments
- Develop and present White Papers with practical regional recommendations

## КОМУ ПОДХОДИТ КУРС (Who Is This Course For)
- Logistics companies and operators
- Exporters and manufacturing enterprises
- Government structures and development institutions (QazTrade, KazExport, CAREC, ministries and akimats)
- Universities and research centers (teachers, undergraduates, PhD students, analysts)
- Donors, banks, and analytical organizations (ADB, AIIB, EBRD, UNDP, UNESCAP, GIZ, OECD)

## МОДУЛИ КУРСА (Course Modules)
1. Central Asia's geoeconomic role in BRI
2. Regional countries: challenges and potential
3. Infrastructure and transport corridors
4. Kazakhstan as sustainable development hub
5. Risks and development scenarios to 2035
6. Financing and partnerships (PPP, blended finance, ESG)
7. Final project — White Paper 2035

## Course Title
BRI LOGISTICS: INFRASTRUCTURE, RISKS AND SCENARIOS OF DEVELOPMENT CENTRAL ASIA

## Description
A strategic course for specialists, managers and analysts of international logistics in Central Asia.

Aims to help participants form an international competence system in Central Asia in the field of analysis, design and management of logistics corridors and export routes, based on the standards of ADB, AIIB, EBRD, OECD, UNESCAP and the principles of the OECD Learning Compass 2030.

## Format
- Duration: 24 academic hours
- Format: Hybrid (lectures, case studies, simulations, project work)
- Level: Professional, for managers, analysts, experts and teachers
- Certification: Confirms professional growth and willingness to work internationally

## WHAT WILL YOU LEARN?
- Own the tools for evaluating route effectiveness (ROI, KPI, ESG Dashboard)
- Design logistics routes considering risks, infrastructure and tariffs
- Analyze route effectiveness and development scenarios through 2035
- Apply ESG approaches and blended finance tools
- Develop and present a White Paper with practical recommendations for the region

## WHO IS SUITABLE FOR THE COURSE
- Logistics companies and operators: owners, project managers, supply chain directors
- Exporters and manufacturing enterprises: heads of foreign economic activity, market development managers
- Government structures and development institutions: QazTrade, KazExport, CAREC, ministries and akimats
- Universities and research centers: teachers, undergraduates, PhD students, analysts
- Donors, banks and think tanks: ADB, AIIB, EBRD, UNDP, UNESCAP, GIZ, OECD

## COURSE MODULES
1. The geo-economic role of Central Asia in the BRI initiative
2. Countries of the region: challenges and potential
3. Infrastructure and transport corridors
4. Kazakhstan as a hub of sustainable development
5. Risks and scenarios of development until 2035
6. Financing and partnerships (PPP, blended finance, ESG)
7. Final draft — White Paper 2035
